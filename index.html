Neural Network

多分类输出

以四分类为例:

$$\begin{bmatrix}1\0\0\0\end{bmatrix},\begin{bmatrix}0\1\0\0\end{bmatrix},\begin{bmatrix}0\0\1\0\end{bmatrix},\begin{bmatrix}0\0\0\1\end{bmatrix}$$

分别表示样本预测结果为第1,2,3,4种分类

损失函数

Logistic regression的损失函数:

$$J(\theta) = - \frac {1}{m} [\sum\limits\{i=1}^{m}y^{(i)}\log h\{\theta}(x^{(i)})+(1-y^{(i)})\log (1-h\{\theta}(x^{(i)}))] + \frac {\lambda}{2m} \sum\limits\{j=1}^{n}\theta\_{j}^{2}$$

Neural Network的损失函数:

$$J(\theta) = - \frac {1}{m} [\sum\limits\{i=1}^{m}\sum\limits\{k=1}^{K}y\k^{(i)}\log (h\{\theta}(x^{(i)})\k)+(1-y\_k^{(i)})\log (1-h\{\theta}(x^{(i)})\k)] + \frac {\lambda}{2m} \sum\limits\{l=1}^{L-1}\sum\limits\{i=1}^{s\_l}\sum\limits\{j=1}^{s\{l+1}}(\theta\{ji}^{(l)})^2$$

Neural Network的损失函数与Logistic regression的只基本一直, 只因为Neural Network的输出是一个K维向量(K为分类的类别数), 所以要把K维的每一维都计算一遍.
